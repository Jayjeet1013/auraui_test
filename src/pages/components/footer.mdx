## Aura Footer Component

The AuraFooter component is a comprehensive footer designed for all platforms. It features multiple sections for easy navigation, including company information, help resources, additional links, and social media icons. This footer ensures users can quickly access important pages such as About, Features, Customer Support, and Terms & Conditions. The footer also includes branding and copyright information, making it a complete solution for professional sites.
