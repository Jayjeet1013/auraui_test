Let's take a closer look at some of the key components available in Arura UI:

1. **Header Component:** Start your project off right with our customizable header component. Choose from various layouts and styles to create a header that sets the tone for your interface.

2. **Footer Component:** Complete your design with our versatile footer component. From simple footers to more complex layouts, Arura UI offers options to suit every need.

3. **Hero Section:** Make a bold statement with our hero section component. Showcase your key content or message prominently at the top of your page, grabbing users' attention from the get-go.

4. **Stats Display:** Highlight important statistics and metrics with our stats display component. Whether you're showcasing business data or performance metrics, this component makes it easy to present information in a visually appealing manner.

5. **Pricing Section:** Monetize your product or service effectively with our pricing section component. Display pricing plans, features, and benefits in a clear and compelling format, helping users make informed decisions.
